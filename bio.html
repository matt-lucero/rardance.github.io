Bio

Rebecca Rubenstein is a dancer, choreographer and teacher.  She started her dance training at 3 years old in Connecticut.  She received her Bachelor of Arts Degree in Modern Dance from Keene State College in 2004.  After college, Rebecca moved to California and started choreographing and teaching professionally. She then moved to the San Francisco Bay Area in 2008, where she currently teaches and choreographs professionally for multiple companies.  Although choreography is her passion, she loves the joy performing brings.  Through teaching a diverse range of students, her life has been greatly enriched.  Rebecca strongly believes in the spirit of dance that lives within each individual.
